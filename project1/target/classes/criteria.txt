Pizzeria website

You need to create a website for a local pizzeria
a customer should be able to create a pizza loyalty account
they can order a pizza pickup or delivery and have reward points added to their account (money for the order can be assumed to be paid on arrival or when delivered)
they should be able to redeem reward points 
A customer can see the status of their order (pending, ready for pickup or out for delivery, complete)

An employee should be log in 
They should be able to see active orders
They should be able to update order status

Bonus- 
Employees can see a statistics page showing stats about what pizzas are ordered the most or what peak times are etc
customers have a build a custom pizza feature
customers save orders to their account for easy ordering
customers can see a history of their orders
